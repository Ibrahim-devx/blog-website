About Me

This is what I do.

I am a passionate Mechatronics Engineer and Full-Stack Developer with a strong focus on robotics, AI systems, and smart technologies. My journey in technology began with a fascination for automation and embedded systems, and it has since evolved into a career dedicated to building innovative solutions that solve real-world problems.

Over the years, I have worked on a variety of projects, from developing intelligent robots to creating secure web applications. My expertise lies in Python, Flask, Node.js, and embedded systems, and I am constantly exploring new tools and technologies to enhance my skills. Whether it's designing a color-based sorting robot or building a real-time security system for gold stores, I thrive on turning ideas into reality.

When I'm not coding or tinkering with hardware, I enjoy sharing my knowledge through blogging and contributing to open-source projects. I believe in the power of technology to make the world a better place, and I am committed to creating solutions that are not only efficient but also reliable and user-friendly. Let's build the future together!
